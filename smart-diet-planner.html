      Daily Health Tips

      Small consistent habits that compound into lifelong health.

      💧

      Stay Hydrated

      Drink 8–10 glasses of water every day. Water flushes toxins, boosts your metabolism, and keeps your energy levels steady all day long.

      🥗

      Eat the Rainbow

      Fill half your plate with colourful vegetables. Every colour delivers unique vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants your body needs to thrive.

      🚶

      Walk 30 Min Daily

      A brisk 30-minute walk reduces heart disease risk by up to 30%, burns 150+ calories, and powerfully boosts mental clarity and mood.

      😴

      Sleep 7–8 Hours

      Quality sleep regulates hunger hormones (leptin & ghrelin). Chronic sleep deprivation is directly linked to weight gain and low energy.

      🍽️

      Eat Mindfully

      Eat slowly and avoid screens during meals. Your brain takes 20 minutes to register fullness — slow down and enjoy every bite.

      🧘

      Manage Stress

      High cortisol promotes belly fat storage. Even 10 minutes of daily deep breathing or meditation makes a measurable health difference.
